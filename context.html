Growing up as one of the few Asian students in my middle and high school, I never felt like I fit in.

I was often picked on for my race and appearance.

While teachers assumed I was smart, my classmates labeled me as an overachiever.

I was scared to tell anyone about my emotional struggles in school, afraid that I would seem weak or incapable of being independent.

All this emotional suppression over the years had made me a very sensitive person.

I overthought everything, even simple tasks like crossing a street or entering a building.

I stressed over the smallest things, always wondering if others were judging me.

This thesis project is not just a reflection for my past self, but a message to anyone who feels alone, unseen, or overwhelmed.
